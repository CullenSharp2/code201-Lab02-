About Me

My name is Skyler Johnson, I am 37 years of age, and I live in Spokane, WA. I was born in Billings, MT and grew up in Eugene, OR.

I ended up moving to Spokane, WA in 2015 when I won the audition for bass trombone in the Spokane Symphony Orchestra. I am currently still a member of the orchestra and I teach part-time at Whitworth University.

The pandemic has devastated the performing arts industry and will take a long time to recover. I am taking my skills and tool I learned as a musician and applying them to my new journey, becoming a software developer. My goal is to work hard in this field and do my best.
